Sustainable Materials

Bamboo is one of the ancient buildingmaterial used by people.
It is found easily in all countries and has ability to grow in harsh climatic conditions.

India is well known in the world for its abundant bamboo resources.

It has special features that give it a great boost to be used in the construction industry.

It is a good substitute for wood resources as they are vanishing and restrictions are there on falling of trees.

This will be a great step towards sustainable development as concrete causes more pollution and uses more energy.

It meets the sustainability levels due to: light weight, little impact on nature, resistances, etc. The paper discusses an overview of potential properties of bamboo, solution for various roadblocks for its adaptation, strength comparison, etc.

Growth parameters post Covid 19 are also discussed.
